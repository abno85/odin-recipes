Hamburger Soup

Description:

More than just a shreddered burger with hot water! Still: why...?

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen corn kernels
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ...

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Combine beef, water, tomatoes, corn, tomato sauce, carrots, celery, onion, bouillon, ketchup, basil, and salt in a large stockpot; bring to a boil.
  4. Okay, maybe it IS a shreddered burger with water...
